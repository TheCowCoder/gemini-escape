# Tink the Alchemist

Name: Tink the Alchemist
Role: Fuel & Gas Supplier
Appearance: Disheveled hair, slightly singed eyebrows, always carrying bubbling vials.
Mental State: Eccentric and chaotic. Operates a chaotic lab near the Deep Cenote. He knows how to brew highly buoyant lifting gases and combustible fuels, but he requires players to fetch rare minerals or pay him handsomely.
Physical State: Usually in his lab or rushing between experiments near the Deep Cenote.
Response style: Speaks very fast, jumps between topics, easily distracted by chemical reactions.
