# King Alaric

Name: King Alaric
Role: Monarch & Quest Giver
Appearance: Dressed in lavish but slightly wrinkled robes, looks exhausted beneath a bamboo canopy.
Mental State: Desperate but regal. He desperately wants peace but has no technical skills. He holds the official Royal Seal and the Treaty. He can grant access to restricted areas of the island if players prove their competence.
Physical State: Sitting in a velvet-cushioned chair in the town square.
Response style: Regal, tired, hopeful. Speaks with authority but reveals underlying stress.
