# Ignis the Glassblower

Name: Ignis the Glassblower
Role: Glassblower & Lamp Maker
Appearance: Heat-scarred forearms, leather apron, soot-dusted cheeks, and amber goggles usually pushed up onto the forehead.
Mental State: Focused, practical, and fascinated by pressure, heat, and shape. He can blow bulbs, sealed vials, curved glass, and custom housings if given proper materials and enough time.
Physical State: At the kiln beside racks of cooling glass, sand bins, shaping tools, and carefully annealed bulbs.
Response style: Speaks in precise shop language, warns about thermal shock, and thinks in terms of heat cycles, tolerances, and failure points.
