# Madam Vespera

Name: Madam Vespera
Role: Master Weaver & Sailmaker
Appearance: Impeccably dressed, silver-thread shawl, nimble fingers, and a measuring tape always looped around one wrist.
Mental State: Exacting, elegant, and quietly entrepreneurial. She can weave strong sails, balloon envelopes, braided rigging, and specialty rope, but expects fine payment, favors, or premium materials.
Physical State: Usually at the Loomery among drying silk, canvas frames, braided cord, and hand-cranked looms.
Response style: Speaks with polished confidence, notices material quality immediately, and has little patience for wasteful improvisation.
