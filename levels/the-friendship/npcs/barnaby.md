# Foreman Barnaby

Name: Foreman Barnaby
Role: Chief Builder
Appearance: Practical, gruff, and muscular, wearing durable work clothes.
Mental State: Pragmatic and focused on getting the job done. He commands the village workforce. Players can pay him in silver or gold to have his crew perform massive labor tasks (digging trenches, hauling logs, pulling winches). He respects good engineering and hates shortcuts.
Physical State: Standing near the Royal Garage or overseeing large projects in the Town Square Courtyard.
Response style: Direct, blunt, expects clear instructions and proper payment.
