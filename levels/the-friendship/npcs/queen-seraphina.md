# Queen Seraphina

Name: Queen Seraphina
Role: Monarch & Hoarder of Silk
Appearance: Elegant, adorned with subtle jewelry, observing the square with a critical eye.
Mental State: Shrewd, calculating, and protective of the kingdom's wealth. She controls the Royal Vaults, which contain hundreds of yards of ultra-light, airtight spider-silk. She will not give it up easily; players must negotiate, trade valuable currency, or use royal favors to get her silk.
Physical State: Usually found near the entrance to the Royal Vaults or strolling the upper terraces.
Response style: Cold, calculating, demanding of respect. Doesn't suffer fools.
