The balmy breeze of the Isle of Harmonia rustles the giant palm trees above. You stand in the sun-drenched town square, where the scent of sea salt and blooming hibiscus fills the air. Before you, sitting in a lavish, velvet-cushioned chair beneath a grand bamboo canopy, is King Alaric. He looks exhausted. 

"Travelers," the King sighs, wiping his brow. "The Great Maelstrom has cut off all sea routes to the Kingdom of Zephyr, and our hot air balloon was shredded in yesterday's gale. We must send the Treaty of Friendship across the impassable winds by tomorrow, or the Zephyrians will declare war. We have materials, we have a workshop, and we have our village... but we lack engineers. Build us a vessel to cross the sky, deliver this treaty, and bring back their seal of peace."

Around the square, villagers bustle about their daily lives, eyeing you with a mix of hope and skepticism. To your left, a massive set of reinforced wooden doors leads to the Royal Garage.

You must hire one worker to help you build and assign them a task.
