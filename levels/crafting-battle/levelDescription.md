The player stands at the entrance of a large battle hall. Inside is a preparation room filled with tools, machines, hanging materials, kitchenware, heavy objects, and strange useful junk. A digital sign names each upcoming opponent and gives the player a chance to invent gear before each round. After every victory, the crafting room opens again with new context for the next challenge. Opponents escalate from a rider on an armored hog to archers, a giant cyclops, a group of men, a wise old man with a question, a tank, a water pit with a bull shark, and a final dramatic showdown.
