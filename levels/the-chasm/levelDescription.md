The player is on a cliff facing a wide chasm.
About four meters across is another cliff with land.
A man is standing on the other side, watching you with concentration.
At his feet is a stake anchored to the ground.

On the player's side of the cliff, there is a small house. It is fully stocked. Let it be user's little imagination paradise to create wonderful inventions and potions but not to enable a god mode or take away from the challenge of the level. And it shouldn't be like a magic generator, no. User should have to hierarchically explore and find things. Give them little hints as they go from the moment they enter the house and smell the scent of parchment (the potion book) and herbs (an ingredients drawra).

Inside the house there is:
- A desk with a knife
- A drawer with a shovel, a saw, and a long rope
- A bookshelf full of old books including a potion book
- A cabinet with a pot and cup
- A stove and sink with running water

Outside the house there is:
- A sturdy oak tree near the cliff edge
- Several loose rocks of various sizes (one geode that's super rare)
- Soft dirt
- A frog near a damp patch
- Earthworms hidden beneath the soil
